Meet the Chef

Chef Ava Sanchez is the culinary genius behind Recipyee! Born and raised in Charlotte, North Carolina, Ava developed a love for cooking at a young age. She curd, Jeff, their two children, Amy and Bob, and their beloved husky, Fluffy. She has strong a passion for cooking, Ava pursued her education at top culinary schools across the country. Her dedication and skill earned her a prestigious Michelin star, and she now owns two highly rated restaurants in Charlotte and Atlanta.

Mission Statement

At Recipyee, Chef believes that eating clean should never mean losing flavor. Our mission is to create healthier living by providing yummy and nutritious recipes. Chef wants the world to be a place where people take better care of their bodies while still enjoying the joy of food. Cooking is fun!
